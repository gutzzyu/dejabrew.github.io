Our Story

Deja Brew began with the idea of opening a cafe that provides a warm and welcoming environment for coffee and food enthusiasts. The owners were inspired by the unique cafes in Korea, such as Nuldam Space Cafe, which offered customers the opportunity to write letters to their future selves as a tangible keepsake of their memories and experiences. As stated by The Smart Local Korea, "writing a letter to your future self is not so bad after all" (Chia, 2019). The owners, however, were aware of the cultural differences between the Philippines and Korea and were uncertain how the trend would be received by Filipino consumers. Despite this uncertainty, they were determined to offer this unique experience to their customers at Deja Brew.
